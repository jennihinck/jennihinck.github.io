Writing

Jenni is a jack-of-all-trades writer, though she focuses primarily on fiction. She has had several short stories and poems published, and was the winner of the 2012 New Play Festival at Concordia University Chicago for her ten-minute play: The Haberdashery of Thaddeus Pellegrin.
