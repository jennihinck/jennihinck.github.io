About

Jenni is a rare Minnesotan who actually likes the cold and snow. She enjoys good stories in all forms (books, movies, TV, video games, podcast, etc), collecting and playing instruments (badly), cross stitching (very slowly), and crocheting (slightly less slowly). She is working on expanding her collection of published writing and her web design portfolio, as well as her collection of swords.

Education

Jenni studied theatre and creative writing at Concordia University Chicago, graduating magna cum laude with a B.A. in Theatre in 2013. She also studied web design at the University of Michigan via Coursera.
