Sourdoughnuts

The Yukon's Gourmet Pop Up Doughnut Shop! Founded in 2017 by Sarah Hamilton and Fiona McTaggart, Sourdoughnuts is an artisan pop up doughnut shop located in Whitehorse, Yukon. We specialize in combination sourdough yeast doughnuts and offer new delicious flavours every pop up. Check out our website!

The Sweeties

The Sweeties band is made up of a pair of real-life sweeties – Patrick Hamilton and Fiona McTaggart, who live together in Whitehorse, Yukon. Though The Sweeties is a relatively new creation for the two, Patrick and Fiona have been playing music together since they met, more than ten years ago. Together their joint love for traditional old time and bluegrass music, coupled with an insatiable desire for good rock and roll, comes together in The Sweeties like the perfect storm. Check out our website for more info!
