About

Fiona McTaggart has lived all over Canada but currently calls Whitehorse home way up in the Yukon. She spends her time writing, reading, and keeping the fire going in her little Yukon cabin.

When she's not writing you can find her hunched over the deep-fryer at her doughnut pop up shop, Sourdoughnuts, or drowning in scrap fabric in front of her sewing machine. She has an Honours BA in English and Creative Writing from Concordia University in Montreal and is a staff writer for MuggleNet.com.
