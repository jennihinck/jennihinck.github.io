Theatre

Jenni has been involved in theatre from a very early age. She has appeared as an actor in over 15 productions, as well as working as an assistant director, playwright, assistant stage manager, master electrician, and assistant technical director. While studying theatre at Concordia University Chicago, she won the Golden Drill award for technical theatre and the 2012 New Play Festival.

Music

Jenni has been making music all her life. She started playing piano in first grade and flute in fourth, adding guitar and drums in high school. She has since added bass, mandolin, violin, cello, and clarinet to her collection of instruments and can play them all with varying degrees of success. She also sings, and was in several choirs and other singing groups throughout high school and college.

Baking

Jenni enjoys baking both at home and professionally. She has worked for Panera Bread, Rustica Bakery, and Nothing Bundt Cakes. She likes having fun while baking and making imaginative creations. Her Lord of the Rings inspired cake was featured twice in the Star Tribune article about the 2017 Edible Book Festival at the Minneapolis Institute of Art.
